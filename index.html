What is a first aid kit?

A first aid kit is a collection of supplies and equipment that is used to give medical treatment.There is a wide variation in the contents of first aid kits based on the knowledge and experience of those putting it together.

First Aid Burn Relief Kits

This is a mobile First Aid Kit with products for handling burns.For example there can be a Burn Gel products that provides cooling and pain relief for first aid and second degree burns.

First Aid Emergency Kits

A small kit bix containing items such as bandages,plasters and anti septic wipes for use in giving help to an injured person.

First Aid Travel Kits

This is a kit used when people use it while travelling.
